Our Story

Who We Are

Born in France,
Rooted in Rwanda

Maison Dorée was born from a single, unwavering conviction: that the world's greatest cuisine deserves a home wherever people are ready to embrace it. Our founder, Chef Pierre Renard, fell in love with Rwanda during a visit in 2022 and saw in Kigali's rising energy the perfect city to introduce authentic French fine dining to East Africa.

Opening in 2025, Maison Dorée Kigali is the first of its kind — a restaurant dedicated to bringing the full artistry of French classical cooking to Rwandan diners, crafted with the same reverence and technique you would find in the finest tables of Paris, Lyon or Bordeaux, yet rooted in the warmth of Rwandan hospitality.

"Rwanda's soil, its produce, its warmth — they reminded me of Burgundy in its soul. I knew this was where Maison Dorée had to grow next."

— Pierre Renard, Founder & Executive Chef

Pierre leads a team of classically trained chefs who moved to Kigali with a shared mission: to make French cuisine approachable, joyful and deeply memorable for Rwandan guests and international visitors alike.

Our Values

Seasonality First

Our menu changes with the harvest. Every dish is a reflection of its moment in time — never compromising freshness for convenience.

Sourcing with Integrity

We source directly from Rwandan hill farms and carefully import French specialities — cheeses, wines, truffles — for authenticity on every plate.

Hospitality as Care

We treat each guest as a dear friend — with genuine warmth, attentiveness and the desire for them to leave truly happy.

Continuous Curiosity

We never stop learning, tasting, or questioning. Our kitchen is a living laboratory where tradition meets the courage to try something new.
